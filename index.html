How It Works

Our smart system uses a simple but effective decision-making process to keep your plants healthy.

1

Sense

Soil moisture sensor continuously measures water content in the soil

2

Analyze

System compares current moisture level with your set threshold

3

If moisture is below threshold, trigger irrigation; otherwise, wait

4
